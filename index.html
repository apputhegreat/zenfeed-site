What it does

Less noise.
More intention.

Five quiet controls that put you back in charge of what you see on LinkedIn and X.

Zen mode for AI

Take a timed break from AI-heavy LinkedIn and X content. Set 5 min, 1 hour, or longer — Zenfeed filters locally, then steps aside.

Reduce promoted posts

Sponsored, promoted, and ad-labeled feed items are reduced before they reach your scroll. Toggle once — Zenfeed works locally on LinkedIn and X page loads.

Remove suggested posts

Only see content from people you actually chose to follow. "People you may know" and algorithm-driven recommendations vanish from your feed.

Collapse, don't delete

Hidden posts shrink to a slim bar instead of disappearing entirely. Click it any time to peek or restore — full control, no surprises, nothing lost forever.

Block by keyword

Add any word or phrase you're tired of reading. Zenfeed catches every matching post on load — no delays, no page refreshes, no second chances for that word.
