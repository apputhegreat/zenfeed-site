Uninstalled

Zenfeed is gone. One last thing.

If you still have LinkedIn or X open, refresh that tab and your feed goes back to normal. Everything Zenfeed hid comes straight back.

Refresh your LinkedIn or X tab

Press Cmd + R (Mac) or Ctrl + R (Windows) on any LinkedIn or X tab you had open. That's it.

Why is this needed? Zenfeed hid posts on pages that were already open. When you uninstall an extension, Chrome removes it immediately — it doesn't get a moment to put those posts back first. A refresh rebuilds the page from scratch, without Zenfeed, so the feed returns to normal. Nothing was deleted or changed on your account.

Your data is already gone

Zenfeed kept your settings — toggles, keywords, timer — only on your own computer, and never had an account or a server. Uninstalling deleted all of it. There's nothing for you to clear and nothing for us to delete.

If something pushed you out, we'd like to know

Especially if Zenfeed hid something it shouldn't have, missed things you expected it to catch, or a feed looked broken. Those are fixable, and knowing beats guessing.

Head to Support to report it. No account needed.
