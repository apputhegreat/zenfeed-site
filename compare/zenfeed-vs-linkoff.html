Zenfeed vs LinkOff

LinkOff is a free, LinkedIn-only feed customizer with a deep set of content-type filters. Zenfeed covers LinkedIn plus X and YouTube, and adds AI-content detection through its Rest from AI timer. Here's how they actually compare.

Side by side

Feature Zenfeed LinkOff
Price Free, no tiers Completely free, no ads
Platforms LinkedIn, X/Twitter, YouTube LinkedIn only
Account required No Not required
AI-content detection Yes — Rest from AI keyword/phrase sweep, timed None
Promoted + Suggested filtering Yes, toggle per category Yes — promoted post blocking, plus filters for polls, videos, interaction-based posts, old posts, premium upsells, course recommendations, and blocked companies/people
Keyword blocking Yes — custom keyword list Yes — custom keyword filtering
Timed auto-restore Yes — 15/30/60 min or a custom duration, everything comes back automatically when the timer ends No

Why someone might choose Zenfeed

If you spend time on X or YouTube as well as LinkedIn, Zenfeed covers all three from one install rather than needing a separate LinkedIn-only tool. It also adds AI-content detection through Rest from AI, a feature LinkOff doesn't have at all.

Why someone might still want LinkOff

If LinkedIn is the only feed you want to clean up, LinkOff's filtering is more granular for that one platform — it can hide the feed entirely, filter by content type (polls, videos), block specific companies or people, remove old posts, block premium upsells, and block course recommendations, none of which Zenfeed offers. It also runs on any Chromium browser (Brave, Edge, Opera, Vivaldi), not just Chrome.

Source

LinkOff facts above (pricing, platforms, ratings) are from its live Chrome Web Store listing, checked July 23, 2026: 4.0★ (56 ratings), ~3,000 users, updated June 27, 2026.
